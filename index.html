ABOUT ME

A little about me...

Hello, my name is Thaddeus

AGE: 24

LOCATION: Singapore

LANGUAGES: English, Mandarin, German (A1.1), Korean (L1), French (Basic)

I am a final year undergraduate aerospace engineering student at the Nanyang Technological University (NTU).

My journey in this field began when I enrolled for my Diploma in Aeronautical Engineering with Singapore Polytechnic (SP). The 7 years since then has been filled with tons of valuable experiences, bringing me from the classrooms & labs of SP, to a 6-week Overseas Industrial Attachment with AMECO, Beijing and finally to NTU, where I also had the opportunity to embark on a semester long GEM Explorer exchange programme with the University of Sheffield in United Kingdom -- of course, completing my 2-year National Service too

Fueled by a passion for innovation and a love for all things that soar, let's just say, my head's always been in the clouds -- in the best way possible! As I anticipate my graduation, I also look forward to contributing to the advancement of aerospace technology and research!
